About

Nutrillent is a USDA-based online nutrient tracker targetted towards those suffering from vitamin and mineral deficiencies. Deficiencies are some of the most common healthcare issues in the world today, with over two billion people worldwide suffering from at least one vitamin or mineral deficiency. Among these, iron, zinc, iodine, vitamin A, and vitamin B12 are the most prevalent. At Nutrillent, our goal is to tackle malnourishment and help you ensure you are getting the nutrition you need, without the need for pills and medication.

With Nutrillent Tracker, you can track the food you eat and discover if you are meeting your vitamin and mineral goals each day. Our extensive database covers any kind of food available, making it easy to find out if you are within your nutrient goals.
